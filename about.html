About the Provider

Meet Eduke Roline Melle, PMHNP-BC
Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner
Licensed in Maryland & Washington, D.C.



Compassionate Care Rooted in Excellence

Eduke Roline Melle is a board-certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP-BC) with a passion for providing holistic, evidence-based mental healthcare. With extensive clinical experience and specialized training, she brings a unique perspective that bridges mental and physical wellness.
Her approach to psychiatric care is deeply rooted in compassion, cultural sensitivity, and the belief that every individual deserves personalized treatment that honors their unique journey and circumstances. She specializes in treating a wide range of mental health conditions including anxiety, depression, ADHD, eating disorders, and psychotic disorders.
Roline is committed to creating a safe, non-judgmental environment where patients feel heard, understood, and empowered to take an active role in their healing process.


Specialties

Her clinical specialties includes the following:

  • Anxiety Disorders
  • ADHD
  • Depression & Major Depressive Disorder (MDD)
  • Mood Disorders
  • Eating Disorders
  • Psychosis & Psychotic Disorders

    • In addition to psychiatric care, Roline manages chronic physical conditions including:

  • Diabetes
  • Hypertension
  • Hyperlipidemia (High Cholesterol)

She also provides STD testing, routine lab work, and treatment for acute illnesses such as sinus infections and upper respiratory infections (URIs), ensuring continuity between mental and physical health care.

Approach to Care

What sets Roline's practice apart is the blended model of care she offers. She integrates psychotherapy with evidence-based medication management, while also addressing physical health needs. This allows her to support patients in a holistic, balanced, and safe way, treating more than just symptoms, but the full story behind them.
In early sessions, patients can expect a nonjudgmental, welcoming space where concerns are explored, health history is reviewed, and a customized treatment plan is developed based on individual goals.

Empowerment Through Compassion

Roline believes deeply that everyone deserves access to thoughtful, high-quality care, care that is grounded in empathy, driven by collaboration, and centered on healing.

Her mission is to provide accessible, compassionate, and evidence-based psychiatric care that empowers individuals to achieve their highest potential for mental wellness and committed to creating a therapeutic environment where healing, growth, and transformation can flourish.
