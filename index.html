Ronest MindSpring Psychiatry & Wellness Clinic

An Overview

At Ronest MindSpring Psychiatry & Wellness Clinic, we provide integrated psychiatric care that recognizes the close connection between mental and physical health. Our mission is to treat the whole person—mind and body, through compassionate, personalized, and evidence-based services.

About the Provider

Eduke Roline Melle, PMHNP-BC
Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner Licensed in Maryland and Washington, D.C
Roline is a compassionate and highly qualified provider with board certification in Psychiatric Mental Health. She specializes in diagnosing and treating a wide range of conditions, including anxiety, ADHD, depression, major depressive disorder (MDD), mood disorders, eating disorders, and psychosis.
Her approach integrates psychotherapy and medication management, ensuring that treatment is holistic and tailored to the unique needs of each individual.

Eduke Roline Melle
PMHNP-BC

Why Ronest MindSpring?

  • Mental health and physical health are deeply connected:
    Chronic medical conditions can worsen psychiatric symptoms and vice versa.
  • Holistic treatment improves outcomes:
    Treating psychiatric needs leads to better symptom control and quality of life.
  • Personalized care:
    Treatments are adapted to the individual's unique physical and mental health profile, enhancing safety and effectiveness.
  • Supports lifespan care:
    Services are designed for all ages, addressing the evolving needs across the lifespan.
